## MyInterest – Social Interest Sharing Platform

### Overview
MyInterest is a web-based social platform designed to help users share their interests and discover others with similar passions. Unlike traditional social networks where users mainly post personal status updates, MyInterest organizes content around interests, topics, and trends—allowing users to connect, explore, and stay updated on what matters to them. The platform encourages people to share, connect and explore their interests.

### My Role
**Full-Stack Developer**

### Tech Stack
- **Front-End:** React
- **Back-End:** Node.js, Express
- **Database:** PostgreSQL

### Key Features
- **User Authentication:** Login, logout, and secure sign-up flow.
- **Interest Feed:** Displays the latest posts based on topics and user preferences.
- **Search System:** Search posts, users, and topics with real-time suggestions.
- **Explore Section:** Discover trending content, popular topics, and active communities.
- **Social Interaction:** Like, comment, follow users, send friend requests, and manage friend lists.
- **Notifications:** Real-time alerts for new friend requests, post interactions, and system updates.
- **Profile Management:** Update profile info, avatar, bio, personal interests, and visibility settings.
- **Post Management:** Create, edit, delete posts, and organize topics.
- **Real-Time Chat:** 1-to-1 messaging with live updates and conversation history.
- **Settings Panel:** Manage account details, privacy controls, notification preferences, and interface preferences.

### Challenges
- Designing an interest-based content organization system rather than traditional chronological feeds.
- Implementing real-time chat and notifications with efficient WebSocket handling.
- Ensuring responsive performance when querying trending topics and search results.
- Balancing user privacy with discoverability for social interactions.

### The Result
MyInterest successfully created an engaging environment where users could explore and share their passions while forming meaningful connections. The platform delivered a personalized and dynamic content experience, offering a more focused alternative to mainstream social networks.
