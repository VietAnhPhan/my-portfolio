### Overview
SeaKaren is a water transportation booking platform that enables users to reserve on-demand or scheduled water taxi services across coastal and island regions. The system connects passengers with licensed boat operators, allowing flexible travel beyond fixed ferry timetables. The platform supports route selection, dynamic pricing, real-time availability, and booking management for both travelers and boat service providers.

### Key Features

### Challenges
- Handling dynamic route pricing due to varying distances and seasonal demand.
- Managing real-time updates where weather or sea conditions could impact travel schedules.
- Ensuring a smooth booking workflow across multiple user roles (passenger, operator, admin).
- Maintaining system reliability in areas with unstable coastal internet connectivity.

### The Result
The platform simplified marine transportation booking for both tourists and local island communities, reducing reliance on fixed ferry schedules. Boat operators gained an efficient platform to manage reservations, while passengers enjoyed flexible and transparent travel options.
