## WeCharge – EV Charging Management System

### Overview
WeCharge is an EV charging management platform designed to support condominium residences, commercial buildings, and corporate parking facilities in managing shared EV charging stations. The system enables users to authenticate, start charging sessions, and pay based on actual electricity usage. Facility administrators can configure charging policies, view usage history, and manage billing—helping accelerate the adoption of EV infrastructure in shared parking environments.

### Key Features
